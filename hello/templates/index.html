Message From the Director

World over, the most acclaimed universities and institutions boast of having a strong and vibrant alumnus base. The alumni network of IIT Indore forms a very important and significant element to propel the institute towards excellence in all spheres of science, technology, humanity and social sciences, and in producing leaders. IIT Indore has been fortunate, right from its first graduating batch, to have a robust institute- alumni network, which we must all nurture and see its exponential growth in all dimensions. With the very best wishes.
